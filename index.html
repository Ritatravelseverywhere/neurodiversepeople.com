What this assessment covers

Three presentations. One assessment.

The quiz adapts based on your early answers, routing you through the most relevant validated instruments for your profile.

⚡
ADHD

Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder. Difficulty sustaining attention, impulsivity, restlessness, and executive function challenges — often misunderstood as laziness or carelessness.

🧩
Autism (ASC)

Autism Spectrum Condition. Differences in social communication, sensory processing, and a need for routine or predictability — frequently masked or undetected, especially in women.

✦
AuDHD

Co-occurring ADHD and autism. Increasingly recognised as a distinct presentation with unique challenges — where ADHD impulsivity and autistic need for routine directly conflict.

How it works

Adaptive, not one-size-fits-all

The quiz is structured in phases. Your early answers determine which follow-up assessments you receive — so you only answer what's relevant to you.

1
Initial screens

You complete a short Attention & Focus section (6 questions) and a Social & Communication section (10 questions).

2
Adaptive path

Based on your scores, the quiz routes you into ADHD, Autism, or AuDHD follow-up questions — skipping what's not relevant.

3
Deeper assessment

If autism traits are indicated, you're offered a deeper 40-question Life Experiences section covering social connection, language, sensory experience, and routine.

4
Your NeuroProfile

You receive a visual profile with scores, research-based threshold comparisons, and personalised next-step recommendations.

What we assess

Four areas. Original questions. Evidence-based design.

All questions are original content written for this platform, inspired by decades of published neurodevelopmental research. This tool is not affiliated with or a reproduction of any published diagnostic instrument.

Attention & Focus
ADHD trait assessment

Explores attention regulation, impulsivity, hyperactivity, and executive function — the core domains of ADHD in adults. Inspired by WHO-published research into adult attention disorders.

Social & Communication
Autism trait assessment

Explores social communication differences, sensory processing, and perceptual style — core domains of autism spectrum traits in adults. Inspired by Cambridge University research.

Social Strategies
Masking & camouflaging

Explores how much effort you put into adapting, concealing, and compensating in social situations — a key factor in late autism diagnosis, particularly in women. Inspired by UCL research.

Life Experiences
Extended autism assessment

An optional deeper section covering social connection, language and thinking, sensory experience, and routine — across your whole life, not just recently.
