ADHD in adults — what to look for

ADHD in adults often looks very different from the stereotype of a hyperactive child. Many adults with ADHD — particularly women — present primarily with inattentive symptoms that are easy to miss:

  • Chronic difficulty starting tasks, especially ones that feel boring or overwhelming
  • Losing track of time, being consistently late despite trying hard
  • Forgetting appointments, obligations, and where you put things
  • Mind wandering during conversations, meetings, or while reading
  • Starting projects enthusiastically but struggling to finish them
  • Feeling constantly behind, disorganised, or overwhelmed
  • Emotional intensity and sensitivity to rejection or criticism

If several of these feel familiar — and have done since childhood — this free ADHD test is a good starting point.
